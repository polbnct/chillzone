Popular Service WE Offer!

AC Installation

At TotalTemp Solutions, we specialize in the professional installation of air conditioning systems for both residential and commercial properties. Whether you're looking to install a new split system, a ductless mini-split, or a central air conditioning unit, we ensure that the right system is selected based on the size, layout, and energy needs of your space. Our team of experts will work with you to recommend the most energy-efficient solutions, providing long-term comfort while helping you manage energy costs. We take pride in offering flawless installations that maximize the performance and lifespan of your new air conditioning system.

AC Maintenance

Regular maintenance is key to extending the life of your air conditioning system and keeping it running at peak efficiency. Our comprehensive AC maintenance plans include everything from system tune-ups, cleaning of coils, and checking refrigerant levels to replacing filters and inspecting all components for wear and tear. With our preventative care, we ensure your air conditioning system operates smoothly throughout the year, preventing costly repairs and energy inefficiencies. By keeping your system in optimal shape, we help maintain a comfortable indoor environment while reducing the risk of unexpected breakdowns.

AC Repair Services

If your air conditioning system is not performing as it should, TotalTemp Solutions is here to help. We provide quick and reliable diagnostic services to pinpoint the exact issue, whether it’s a refrigerant leak, faulty motor, damaged compressor, or malfunctioning thermostat. Our experienced technicians are equipped to repair all types of air conditioning systems, using high-quality parts and industry-leading tools to restore your unit to its full functionality. Whether your system is making strange noises, failing to cool effectively, or has stopped working altogether, we offer swift, efficient repairs to get you back to enjoying optimal comfort.

AC Replacement & Upgrades

If your current air conditioning system is outdated, inefficient, or no longer meets your needs, it might be time for an upgrade. Our team will guide you through the entire process of selecting and installing a new energy-efficient system that best fits your space. We also offer expert advice on upgrading to more modern units, including the installation of smart thermostats for greater control over your energy usage. Replacing your old system with a new one can reduce energy costs, improve comfort, and minimize the need for costly repairs down the road. We ensure a seamless replacement process, from selecting the right system to professional installation.
