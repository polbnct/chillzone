Frequently Asked Questions

What types of air conditioning systems do you sell?

We offer a wide range of air conditioning systems to suit both residential and commercial needs, including central air systems, split systems, ductless mini-split systems, and portable air conditioners. Our team will help you choose the right system based on your space size, energy efficiency preferences, and budget.

Can I install an air conditioner myself, or should I hire a professional?

We strongly recommend hiring a licensed professional for installation. Proper installation ensures your system runs efficiently and safely. Incorrect installation can lead to system malfunctions, poor energy efficiency, or even voiding the warranty.

Are your air conditioning systems energy-efficient?

Yes, we offer a wide variety of energy-efficient air conditioners that meet the latest energy standards. We focus on providing systems with high SEER (Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio) ratings to help you save on energy costs and reduce your carbon footprint.

How often should I have my air conditioning system serviced?

We recommend scheduling professional maintenance for your air conditioning system at least once a year, ideally in the spring before peak cooling season. Regular maintenance helps improve system performance, prevent breakdowns, and extend the life of your unit.

Do you offer emergency AC repair services?

Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency AC repair services. If your air conditioner breaks down unexpectedly, especially during extreme weather, we can dispatch a technician to assess and repair your system quickly.

Why is my air conditioner not cooling properly?

Several factors could cause poor cooling, including low refrigerant levels, a clogged filter, a dirty condenser coil, or mechanical issues. If your AC isn't cooling properly, we recommend scheduling a service call to diagnose and fix the issue.

What does AC maintenance include?

Our maintenance service includes cleaning or replacing filters, checking refrigerant levels, cleaning condenser coils, inspecting the compressor and evaporator, and calibrating the thermostat. These steps ensure your system runs efficiently and reduces the risk of major repairs.

Can I upgrade my existing air conditioning system?

Yes! We can upgrade or replace your existing air conditioning system with a more energy-efficient model. Our team will help you choose a system that meets your needs and provides long-term savings on energy bills.
